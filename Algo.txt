1. Decide from which establishment you wish to order dinner.
a. Figure out if you actually want pizza or a calzone.
b. Puzzle out whether you need sides and a soda too.
2. Decide which type of pizza (or calzone, or sando, or salad) to order.
3. Either enter that order into an app like Door Dash, or call the pizzaria.
4. Twiddle your thumbs while you wait for the pie to be delivered.
5. Once the pizza arrives, either:
a. Pay the driver and give them a chunky tip or
b. You've already paid and requested a no-contact delivery. At which point, they drop off the pie and leave.
6. Eat the pizza.
